## Game Mechanics

### Cluster Pays
Connect 5 or more matching symbols horizontally or vertically to form winning clusters:
- 5-9 symbols: 2x - 6x payout
- 10-14 symbols: 5x - 10x payout
- 15-19 symbols: 15x - 25x payout
- 20+ symbols: 50x - 100x payout

### Overdose Cycle (Cascade Feature)
When you win:
1. Winning symbols are removed from the grid
2. New symbols cascade down to fill empty spaces
3. Your multiplier increases by 1x (up to 10x max)
4. Process repeats if new clusters form

### Scatter Rewards
Land scatter symbols to trigger bonuses:
- **3 Scatters**: 100x bet
- **4 Scatters**: 200x bet
- **5+ Scatters**: 500x bet (DETOX SPINS)
